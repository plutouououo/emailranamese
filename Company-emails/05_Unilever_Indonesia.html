Permohonan Kerja Sama KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise

Yth. Pimpinan PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk,

Perkenalkan, kami adalah tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah 2025, sebuah program pengabdian masyarakat dari Universitas Gadjah Mada yang berfokus pada pemberdayaan komunitas melalui pendekatan holistik kesehatan, WASH, pemberdayaan perempuan, dan sustainability di wilayah 3T Raja Ampat.

Kami dari Tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah akan menjalankan program KKN di Desa Jefman Barat dan Samate, Kabupaten Raja Ampat, Distrik Salawati Utara, Papua Barat Daya dengan fokus pada optimalisasi kesehatan masyarakat, sanitasi, kebersihan, pemberdayaan perempuan, serta sustainability initiatives melalui pendekatan Ground-Up Development.

Kami sangat menghargai komitmen PT Unilever Indonesia dalam WASH programs, women empowerment, health & hygiene, environmental sustainability, dan inclusive growth initiatives. Sejalan dengan misi Unilever dalam "Sustainable Living Plan", kami mengajak Unilever untuk turut berkontribusi dalam membangun komunitas yang sehat, bersih, dan berdaya di daerah-daerah yang paling membutuhkan.

📄 Unduh Proposal Partnership Lengkap

Tema Program KKN

"Water, Sanitation, Health, Women Empowerment & Sustainable Living for Community Resilience"

Program yang Selaras dengan Unilever

Program KKN Raja Ampat Berkisah dirancang dengan fokus pada WASH, kesehatan masyarakat, pemberdayaan perempuan, dan environmental sustainability yang sepenuhnya selaras dengan pilar-pilar strategic CSR Unilever dalam Sustainable Living Plan.

💧 1. GEMA SEHAT - Water, Sanitation & Health Program

Program komprehensif mencakup: WASH infrastructure assessment, water quality testing, sanitation facility improvement, handwashing campaigns, dan hygiene education. Kami akan train 300+ health promoters dalam community health awareness including personal hygiene, food safety, dan infectious disease prevention. Perfect alignment dengan Unilever's WASH initiatives & hygiene product promotion strategies. (SDGs 3, 5, 6)

♀️ 2. Women Empowerment & Gender Equality Program

Program pemberdayaan perempuan mencakup: women's health education, maternal & child health, sexual/reproductive health, skill development untuk women's economic independence, dan advocacy against gender-based violence. Kami akan develop 100+ women economic groups melalui UMKM development programs. Directly supports Unilever's commitment ke women's rights, health, dan economic empowerment. (SDGs 3, 4, 5, 8, 10)

♻️ 3. Environmental Sustainability & Plastic Reduction

Program sustainability mencakup: environmental education, waste reduction & plastic bag elimination campaigns, reef/coastal conservation, mangrove restoration, dan community cleanup initiatives. Kami akan promote Unilever's sustainable products & packaging alternatives. Development of community waste management systems & eco-brick initiatives. Supports Unilever's aggressive environmental targets & transition to circular economy. (SDGs 12, 13, 14, 15)

Nilai Strategis Partnership untuk Unilever

Partnership memberikan Unilever: (1) Sustainable Living Plan Delivery - tangible program execution across all four pillars: health & hygiene, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, water security, (2) Market Development - through hygiene promotion campaigns, expand Unilever's market penetration untuk handwashing soaps, shampoos, & health products di remote regions, (3) Brand Loyalty Building - community-level programs build positive brand associations & customer loyalty starting dari grassroots, (4) ESG & Reporting Excellence - provides compelling sustainability narratives & case studies untuk annual sustainability reports backed by academic rigor of UGM partnership, (5) Innovation Testing Ground - pilot Unilever's sustainable product lines & packaging dalam real community settings with feedback loops. Investment CSR Rp 300-400 juta delivers strategic returns: 300+ health promoters as brand ambassadors, 5,000+ new customers for hygiene products, enhanced sustainability metrics, market leadership positioning dalam WASH sector.

Terima kasih atas perhatian PT Unilever Indonesia. Kami yakin partnership ini akan memperkuat positioning Unilever sebagai leader dalam sustainable living sekaligus menciptakan dampak sosial berkelanjutan bagi jutaan keluarga Indonesia.
