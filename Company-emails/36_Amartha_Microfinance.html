Yth. Pimpinan PT Amartha Microfinance,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Amartha untuk partnership dalam UMKM microfinance acceleration & digital financial inclusion di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada UMKM credit access, digital payment adoption, & financial literacy untuk sustainable microenterprise growth.

📄 Lihat Proposal

ARTA NIAGA - UMKM Microfinance & Digital Inclusion

Financial literacy training program melatih 500 UMKM entrepreneurs tentang credit management, business accounting, & Amartha microfinance product features. Microfinance disbursement acceleration program channeling Rp 2+ billion microloans ke 400+ UMKM dengan 90-day working capital cycle. Digital payment integration program training 300 UMKM dalam digital wallet usage & digital transaction for improved financial traceability.

Manfaat Amartha: (1) 400+ UMKM microfinance customers creating Rp 2B+ loan portfolio with supply chain security & repeat lending opportunity, (2) 500 financial-literate UMKM community reducing credit risk & improving loan repayment behavior, (3) 300 UMKM digital payment users creating transaction volume growth & financial inclusion narrative, (4) UMKM growth stories enabling Amartha impact reporting & fintech market expansion. Inclusive finance social impact.
