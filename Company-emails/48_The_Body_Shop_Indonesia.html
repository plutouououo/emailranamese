KARSA TANI & ARTA NIAGA - Natural Beauty Supply Chain & Women Retail

Women beauty retail network establishing 60 retailers creating Rp 2.2+ billion annual ethical beauty product sales dengan sustainability premium.

Manfaat The Body Shop: (1) 80 natural ingredient farmers creating sustainable supply chain & ingredient security with ethical pricing narrative, (2) 60 women beauty retailers creating Rp 2.2B+ annual sales network & ethical beauty values distribution, (3) Sustainable beauty supply chain documenting The Body Shop environmental & women empowerment mission, (4) Natural ingredient network creating competitive sourcing advantage & supply chain traceability. Ethical beauty & sustainable supply chain development.
