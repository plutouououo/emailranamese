Yth. Pimpinan PT Pegadaian (Persero),

Perkenalkan, kami adalah tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah 2025, sebuah program pengabdian masyarakat dari Universitas Gadjah Mada yang berfokus pada financial inclusion, UMKM empowerment, dan peningkatan ekonomi masyarakat di wilayah 3T Raja Ampat.

Kami dari Tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah akan menjalankan program KKN di Desa Jefman Barat dan Samate, Kabupaten Raja Ampat, Distrik Salawati Utara, Papua Barat Daya dengan fokus pada literasi finansial, microfinance access, UMKM empowerment, dan pembangunan economic resilience komunitas.

Kami sangat menghargai komitmen PT Pegadaian dalam financial inclusion for underserved populations, microfinance services, UMKM support, dan poverty alleviation programs. Sejalan dengan misi Pegadaian dalam "Melayani Golongan Menengah ke Bawah", kami mengajak Pegadaian untuk turut berkontribusi dalam membuka akses finansial yang berkelanjutan dan inklusif bagi masyarakat pesisir.

Tema Program KKN

"Financial Inclusion & Microfinance Access for Sustainable Economic Growth"

PROGRAM UNGGULAN RANA MESE MANISE

Program KKN Raja Ampat Berkisah dirancang dengan fokus pada financial literacy, microfinance access, dan UMKM empowerment yang sepenuhnya selaras dengan core mission Pegadaian dalam inclusive financial services.

💼 1. Financial Literacy & Microfinance Education

Program komprehensif mencakup: basic financial management, savings mobilization, understanding credit mechanisms, loan risk assessment, dan responsible borrowing. Kami akan train 400+ community members sebagai financial agents yang dapat promote Pegadaian services. Education tentang Pegadaian's various services (pawn lending, microfinance, insurance) dengan suitability assessment. Direct support untuk Pegadaian's financial inclusion agenda di remote areas. (SDGs 1, 5, 8, 10)

💳 2. ARTA NIAGA - UMKM Financing & Business Development

Program pemberdayaan UMKM mencakup: business plan development, financial projections, collateral alternatives (tangible assets, group guarantee), loan applications prep, dan financial record keeping. Kami akan develop 150+ UMKM yang ready untuk accessing Pegadaian's microfinance products dengan proper collateral structuring. Business coaching untuk improve UMKM viability & loan repayment capacity. Creates bankable UMKM customer base untuk Pegadaian's microfinance portfolio expansion. (SDGs 1, 5, 8, 10)

🏦 3. ARTA NIAGA - Consumer Awareness & Pegadaian Services Access

Program pemaksimalan akses Pegadaian services mencakup: community awareness about Pegadaian service centers, grievance mechanisms, product features comparison, customer rights protection, dan fraud prevention. Kami akan establish community-level customer support centers untuk facilitating Pegadaian services access. Removes access barriers & increases service uptake dalam underserved market segments. (SDGs 1, 8, 10)

Nilai Strategis Partnership untuk Pegadaian

Partnership memberikan Pegadaian: (1) Market Expansion - systematic approach untuk expanding Pegadaian's customer base di underserved islands/regions through community-level education & outreach, (2) Risk Mitigation - financial literacy training reduces default risks by creating informed borrowers dengan proper understanding of obligations, (3) Customer Loyalty - community-based program builds trust & long-term relationships, increasing customer lifetime value & repeat transactions, (4) Operational Support - community financial agents reduce Pegadaian's operational costs dalam reaching remote areas, (5) Corporate Social Responsibility - tangible evidence of financial inclusion mandate & poverty alleviation contribution untuk regulatory & stakeholder accountability. Investment CSR Rp 200-250 juta delivers strategic ROI: 5,000+ new customers accessed, Rp 10+ billion new loan portfolio, enhanced financial inclusion metrics, competitive advantage dalam microfinance market.

Terima kasih atas perhatian PT Pegadaian. Kami yakin partnership ini akan memperkuat Pegadaian's positioning sebagai "The Most Trusted Financial Institution for Millions" sambil menciptakan dampak luas dalam financial inclusion di wilayah 3T.
