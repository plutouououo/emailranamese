KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise Partnership

Yth. Pimpinan PT Sanbe Farma,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Sanbe Farma untuk partnership dalam pharmaceutical access & disease prevention program di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada primary healthcare education, community health worker training, & equitable medicine access untuk improved health outcomes.

📄 Lihat Proposal

GEMA SEHAT - Primary Healthcare & Drug Access Integration

Primary healthcare training melatih 45 community health workers dengan basic disease diagnosis, treatment protocols, & Sanbe pharmaceutical product knowledge. Disease prevention campaign menjangkau 2100+ community members tentang communicable diseases, hygiene practices, & health-seeking behavior change. Community medicine access program establishing 15 community health pharmacies dengan Sanbe product supply ensuring affordable medicine accessibility untuk 8 villages.

Manfaat Sanbe: (1) 45 trained health workers creating community healthcare literacy & pharmaceutical product awareness foundation, (2) 2100+ disease-educated community building health prevention behavior & disease management narrative, (3) 15 community health pharmacy partners creating Sanbe product distribution network & brand presence, (4) Primary healthcare program documentation strengthening Sanbe health security ESG positioning. Pharmaceutical access equity & market expansion in 3T.

Terima kasih. Kami tunggu kerjasama untuk pharmaceutical access dan primary healthcare education.
