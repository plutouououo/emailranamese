KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise Partnership

Yth. Pimpinan PT Ruang Guru,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Ruang Guru untuk partnership dalam education technology accessible di rural areas di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada digital literacy, online learning access, dan teacher capacity development untuk quality education improvement di 3T regions.

📄 Lihat Proposal

LENTERA PELITA - Digital Education Access & Teacher Development

Digital literacy training untuk 400 students & teachers melalui Ruang Guru platform dengan internet connectivity support di 8 schools. Teacher capacity development program melatih 40 teachers dalam blended learning methodology & Ruang Guru content integration. Student learning outcome improvement program mengintegrasikan 600+ students ke Ruang Guru pro subscription dengan Ruang Guru sponsorship support.

Manfaat Ruang Guru: (1) 600+ student subscriptions creating community education market penetration dalam 3T region, (2) 40 trained teachers building Ruang Guru content adoption & quality learning outcomes narrative, (3) Digital education accessibility program strengthening Ruang Guru CSR education mission, (4) Rural learning community asset development extending education technology reach. EdTech inclusion ESG initiative.

Terima kasih. Kami tunggu kerjasama untuk education technology accessibility dan teacher development.
