📄 Lihat Proposal

ARTA NIAGA - Financial Literacy & Housing Microfinance

Financial literacy training menjangkau 1500+ community members tentang savings, insurance, & micro-credit management dengan BTN housing microfinance product education. Housing micro-loan program disbursing 200+ loans dengan target Rp 5+ billion annual lending volume untuk home renewal & basic infrastructure improvement in 8 villages.

Manfaat BTN: (1) 200+ housing microfinance customers creating Rp 5B+ annual lending pipeline with supply security, (2) 1500 community financial education foundation building housing loan product awareness & affordability perception, (3) Housing credit portfolio expansion & NPA risk mitigation through financial literacy, (4) Microfinance social impact documentation for BTN CSR reporting. Financial inclusion ESG strategy.
