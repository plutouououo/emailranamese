Yth. Pimpinan PT Charoen Pokphand Indonesia Tbk,

Perkenalkan, kami adalah tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah 2025, sebuah program pengabdian masyarakat dari Universitas Gadjah Mada yang berfokus pada pemberdayaan agribisnis, peternakan, dan ekonomi komunitas di wilayah 3T Raja Ampat, Papua Barat Daya.

Kami dari Tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah akan menjalankan program KKN di Desa Jefman Barat dan Samate, Kabupaten Raja Ampat, Distrik Salawati Utara, Papua Barat Daya dengan fokus pada optimalisasi pertanian, peternakan, dan pengembangan agribunis berkelanjutan melalui pendekatan Ground-Up Development.

Kami sangat menghargai komitmen PT Charoen Pokphand Indonesia dalam integrated agribusiness development, livestock improvement, sustainable farming practices, dan community partnership programs. Sejalan dengan misi CP dalam "Building Better Life for Millions", kami mengajak CP untuk turut berkontribusi dalam mengembangkan sektor agribisnis lokal yang berkelanjutan dan menguntungkan petani/peternak di daerah-daerah potensial.

Tema Program KKN

"Integrated Agribusiness Development for Sustainable Growth & Community Prosperity"

PROGRAM UNGGULAN RANA MESE MANISE

Program KKN Raja Ampat Berkisah dirancang dengan fokus pada agribisnis terintegrasi mencakup pertanian, peternakan, dan pengembangan ekonomi yang selaras dengan comprehensive agribusiness platform CP.

🌾 1. KARSA TANI + GEMILANG TERNAK - Integrated Agribusiness

Program komprehensif mengintegrasikan pertanian & peternakan mencakup: sustainable farming practices, improved feed production, livestock health management, integrated pest management, dan farm productivity enhancement. Kami akan develop integrated farming systems yang efisien menggabungkan crops & livestock. Focus pada supplying high-quality feed ingredients dari lokal. Direct alignment dengan CP's integrated feed & agribusiness model untuk securing sustainable supply chains. (SDGs 2, 8, 12, 13)

🐓 2. Livestock Productivity & Quality Improvement

Program peningkatan produktivitas ternak mencakup: animal health management, nutrition optimization, breeding practices improvement, disease prevention, dan feed efficiency. Pelatihan pada veterinary basics & farm management untuk local farmer groups. Development of smallholder livestock farming networks yang dapat supply CP's processing facilities. Creates reliable local supplier networks untuk supporting CP's livestock operations expansion. (SDGs 1, 2, 8)

💼 3. Farmer Cooperative & Supply Chain Integration

Program pemberdayaan farmer cooperatives mencakup: organizational development, member training, collective marketing, contract farming preparation, dan quality assurance systems. Direct linkage dengan CP's procurement programs untuk stable market access. Documentation preparation untuk meeting CP's supplier standards. Develops organized farmer/livestock producer groups ready for formal partnerships dengan CP. (SDGs 1, 8, 10, 17)

Nilai Strategis Partnership untuk Charoen Pokphand

Partnership memberikan CP: (1) Supply Chain Resilience - develop diversified supplier bases di new geographic regions, reducing concentration risk, (2) Cost-Effective Farmer Development - KKN subsidizes capacity building & infrastructure development, lowering CP's market entry costs, (3) Integrated Agribusiness Model Testing - real-world ground testing of integrated farming concepts dengan community feedback loops, (4) Sustainable Sourcing Narratives - compelling CSR story about farmer partnerships & sustainable practices untuk sustainability reports & brand building, (5) Partnership Pipeline - identifies & develops farmer groups that become formal CP suppliers & long-term business partners. Investment CSR Rp 350-450 juta delivers strategic ROI: 500+ farmers/400+ livestock producers enrolled, 10+ supplier cooperatives ready for formal partnerships, secured supply pipelines (tons of feed ingredients + livestock annually), enhanced supply chain resilience.

Terima kasih atas perhatian PT Charoen Pokphand. Kami yakin partnership ini akan memperkuat supply chain sustainability sekaligus menciptakan dampak sosial berkelanjutan bagi komunitas agribisnis di wilayah 3T.
