ARTA NIAGA - Digital Transport & Merchant Integration

Digital literacy training untuk 250+ driver & merchant partners dengan Grab driver app, payment system, rating management. Monthly transaction volume target Rp 2.5+ billion dari digital commerce & transportation services di 8 villages.

Manfaat Grab: (1) 250 trained drivers creating sustainable transportation service ecosystem dengan supply security in East Indonesia, (2) 100 merchant partners enabling Grab food/retail services expansion dengan integrated seller base, (3) Digital transaction volume growth creating GMV asset & market penetration in underserved region, (4) Driver & merchant network retention through community development program. Digital inclusion ESG strategy.
