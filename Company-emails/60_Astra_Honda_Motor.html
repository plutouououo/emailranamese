LENTERA PELITA - Motorcycle Dealer & Service Entrepreneur Training

Motorcycle dealer business training melatih 50 entrepreneurs untuk menjadi Honda motorcycle authorized dealers dengan business management & sales training. Motorcycle mechanic certification melatih 80 youth dalam AHM motorcycle repair & maintenance dengan international standard skills. Dealer service network establishing 50 authorized dealers & 80 service centers creating Rp 3.5+ billion annual motorcycle sales & service revenue dengan sustainable entrepreneur employment.

Manfaat Astra Honda Motor: (1) 50 trained dealers creating Rp 3.5B+ annual sales network & motorcycle market penetration in rural NTT, (2) 80 certified mechanics creating aftersales service network & customer support infrastructure, (3) Youth employment & entrepreneurship narrative strengthening AHM youth development & business enablement CSR, (4) Motorcycle dealer ecosystem enabling AHM brand expansion & market leadership in two-wheeler segment. Youth employment & dealer network expansion.
