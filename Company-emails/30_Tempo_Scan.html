Yth. Pimpinan PT Tempo Scan,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Tempo Scan untuk partnership dalam pharmaceutical access & community health program di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada medicine education, disease awareness, dan telemedicine linkage untuk improved healthcare access & medication rationality.

📄 Lihat Proposal

GEMA SEHAT - Pharmaceutical Access & Medicine Education

Disease awareness campaign menjangkau 2000+ community members tentang common health conditions, prevention, & Tempo Scan medicine product education. Medicine education program melatih 40 village health workers dalam medicine rationality, disease management protocols, & telemedicine referral pathways. Pharmacy distributor network merekrut 20 medicine retailers untuk Tempo Scan product distribution creating Rp 1.5+ billion annual pharmaceutical distribution.

Manfaat Tempo Scan: (1) 20 trained pharmacy partners creating Rp 1.5B+ annual distribution network with supply assurance, (2) 2000+ consumer pharmaceutical awareness building Tempo Scan product preference in 3T region, (3) 40 health worker training creating community healthcare literacy & medication rationality, (4) Telemedicine integration strengthening pharmacy-clinical services coordination. Pharmaceutical access ESG expansion.
