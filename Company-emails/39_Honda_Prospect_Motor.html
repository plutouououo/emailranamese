KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise Partnership

Yth. Pimpinan PT Honda Prospect Motor,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Honda untuk partnership dalam youth vocational training & transportation access program di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada motorcycle mechanic training, rural mobility improvement, & youth employment creation untuk sustainable development.

📄 Lihat Proposal

LENTERA PELITA - Motorcycle Mechanic Training & Rural Mobility

Motorcycle mechanic training program melatih 150 youth into motorcycle repair & maintenance with Honda technical standard certification. Youth employment pathway establishing 150 trained mechanics dengan motorcycle service networks across 8 villages. Monthly service volume target Rp 2+ billion dari Honda-certified mechanic network creating youth employment & rural mobility infrastructure.

Manfaat Honda: (1) 150 Honda-certified mechanics creating service network infrastructure & aftermarket revenue stream in NTT, (2) Youth vocational employment creation narrative strengthening Honda youth development CSR position, (3) Rural mobility improved through Honda service accessibility building customer loyalty & brand presence, (4) Mechanic network asset enabling Honda motorcycle penetration & market support. Youth skills & rural mobility development.

Terima kasih. Kami tunggu kerjasama untuk youth training dan rural transportation support.
