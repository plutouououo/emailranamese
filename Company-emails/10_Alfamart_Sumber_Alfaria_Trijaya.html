Permohonan Kerja Sama KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise

Yth. Pimpinan PT Sumber Alfaria Trijaya Tbk (Alfamart),

Perkenalkan, kami adalah tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah 2025, sebuah program pengabdian masyarakat dari Universitas Gadjah Mada yang berfokus pada pemberdayaan UMKM, pengembangan supplier lokal, dan peningkatan ekonomi masyarakat di wilayah 3T Raja Ampat.

Kami dari Tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah akan menjalankan program KKN di Desa Jefman Barat dan Samate, Kabupaten Raja Ampat, Distrik Salawati Utara, Papua Barat Daya dengan fokus pada pemberdayaan UMKM, supply chain development, local supplier empowerment, serta economic resilience komunitas.

Kami sangat menghargai komitmen PT Sumber Alfaria Trijaya (Alfamart) dalam UMKM supplier empowerment, local product sourcing, community economic development, dan inclusive supply chain programs. Sejalan dengan misi Alfamart dalam "Helping Small Businesses Grow", kami mengajak Alfamart untuk turut berkontribusi dalam mengembangkan ecosystem UMKM lokal yang berkelanjutan dan menjadi supplier network Alfamart.

Tema Program KKN

"UMKM Supplier Empowerment & Inclusive Supply Chain Development"

PROGRAM UNGGULAN RANA MESE MANISE

Program KKN Raja Ampat Berkisah dirancang dengan fokus pada UMKM empowerment dan inclusive supply chain development yang sepenuhnya selaras dengan strategic initiatives Alfamart dalam supporting local suppliers.

🏪 1. ARTA NIAGA - UMKM Product Development & Supplier Readiness

Program komprehensif mencakup: product quality improvement, packaging & labeling standards, food safety compliance (untuk product categories applicable), business registration & formal documentation, serta supplier capability assessment. Kami akan develop 200+ UMKM dengan Alfamart supplier potential dalam food, beverages, personal care, and general merchandise categories. Training on Alfamart supplier requirements & certification processes. Direct alignment dengan Alfamart's local supplier sourcing programs. (SDGs 1, 8, 12)

💼 2. ARTA NIAGA - Business Management & Supplier Integration

Program pemberdayaan UMKM mencakup: financial management, inventory control, order fulfillment logistics, quality assurance systems, dan regulatory compliance documentation. Direct engagement dengan Alfamart untuk identifying UMKM dengan supply potential untuk Alfamart stores. Supplier development coaching untuk meeting volume & consistency requirements. Integration preparedness training. Creates qualified local supplier base untuk Alfamart's supply chain expansion. (SDGs 1, 8, 10, 17)

🚚 3. Supply Chain Integration & UMKM Association Development

Program pengembangan supply chain infrastructure mencakup: UMKM association/cluster development, collective buying systems, shared logistics coordination, quality control mechanisms, serta direct supplier-buyer linkages dengan Alfamart. Formalization of supplier groups untuk engaging dengan Alfamart's procurement systems. Capacity building untuk supply chain management. Strengthens Alfamart's supply ecosystem through organized local supplier networks. (SDGs 8, 10, 17)

Nilai Strategis Partnership untuk Alfamart

Partnership memberikan Alfamart: (1) Local Supplier Ecosystem Development - systematic approach untuk identifying, developing, & integrating local UMKM suppliers ke Alfamart's supply chain, supporting Alfamart's "Indonesia Champion" positioning, (2) Product Diversification - local supplier engagement expands Alfamart's product offering dengan local brands & artisanal products, differentiating from competitors, (3) Supply Chain Resilience - diversified local supplier base reduces dependency on centralized suppliers, improving supply chain stability & responsiveness, (4) Community Economic Impact Narrative - tangible CSR story about helping local UMKM grow through Alfamart partnerships, supporting corporate reputation & brand loyalty, (5) Market Intelligence & Expansion Opportunity - community engagement reveals local product demand, growth opportunities, & consumer preferences. Investment CSR Rp 250-350 juta delivers strategic ROI: 200+ UMKM suppliers developed, 50+ new local products for Alfamart shelves, Rp 20+ billion annual local supplier procurement, enhanced supplier reliability, competitive advantage sa inclusive retail model.

Terima kasih atas perhatian PT Sumber Alfaria Trijaya (Alfamart). Kami yakin partnership ini akan memperkuat Alfamart's positioning sebagai champion retailer untuk UMKM sambil menciptakan dampak ekonomi berkelanjutan bagi jutaan bisnis kecil di wilayah 3T.
