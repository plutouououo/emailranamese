Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Lemonilo untuk partnership dalam healthy food security & sustainable agriculture network expansion di Manggarai Timur, NTT. 📄 Lihat Proposal

KARSA TANI & GEMA SEHAT - Organic Farming & Healthy Food Marketplace

Organic farming training program melatih 120 farmers dalam sustainable agriculture, organic certification process, & post-harvest management untuk Lemonilo supply readiness. Healthy food awareness campaign menjangkau 2000+ families tentang nutrition, organic benefits, & healthy eating behavior change. Farm-to-consumer supply chain network establishing 120 farmer suppliers dengan direct Lemonilo marketplace listing creating Rp 2.5+ billion annual farm income.

Manfaat Lemonilo: (1) 120 organic farmer suppliers creating Rp 2.5B+ annual farm supply & supply chain security for healthy food product sourcing, (2) 2000+ health-conscious community building Lemonilo customer base & healthy food adoption narrative, (3) Organic farming network creating sustainable sourcing competitive advantage & brand differentiation, (4) Sustainable agriculture documentation strengthening Lemonilo health & sustainability CSR mission. Farm-to-consumer marketplace expansion.
