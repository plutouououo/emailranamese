Yth. Pimpinan PT Astra Agro Lestari Tbk,

Perkenalkan, kami adalah tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah 2025, sebuah program pengabdian masyarakat dari Universitas Gadjah Mada yang berfokus pada pemberdayaan petani, pengembangan pertanian berkelanjutan, dan optimalisasi sumber daya alam di wilayah 3T Raja Ampat.

Kami dari Tim KKN-PPM UGM Raja Ampat Berkisah akan menjalankan program KKN di Desa Jefman Barat dan Samate, Kabupaten Raja Ampat, Distrik Salawati Utara, Papua Barat Daya dengan fokus pada pengembangan pertanian berkelanjutan, farmer empowerment, optimization of agricultural potential, serta community economic development.

Kami sangat menghargai komitmen PT Astra Agro Lestari dalam sustainable agriculture, farmer development programs, agricultural innovation, dan supporting Indonesia's food security. Sejalan dengan misi Astra Agro dalam "Nourishing the Nation through Sustainable Agriculture", kami mengajak Astra Agro untuk turut berkontribusi dalam mengembangkan potensi pertanian lokal yang berkelanjutan.

Tema Program KKN

"Sustainable Agriculture & Farmer Development for Food Security & Prosperity"

PROGRAM UNGGULAN RANA MESE MANISE

Program KKN Raja Ampat Berkisah dirancang dengan fokus pada sustainable agriculture development dan farmer empowerment yang sepenuhnya selaras dengan core mission Astra Agro dalam mendukung pertanian yang berkelanjutan dan menguntungkan petani.

🌾 1. KARSA TANI - Farmer Development & Agricultural Productivity

Program komprehensif mencakup: implementation of good agricultural practices (GAP), sustainable crop production techniques, soil conservation, integrated pest management, farm productivity improvement, dan crop diversification. Kami akan develop farmer communities yang mengadopsi sustainable practices dengan yield improvement. Demonstration farms showcasing best practices aligned dengan Astra Agro's agricultural knowledge. Direct alignment dengan Astra Agro's farmer development & agricultural extension programs. (SDGs 2, 12, 13)

🚜 2. KARSA TANI - Farmer Input & Technology Access

Program memfasilitasi akses petani ke quality agricultural inputs dan teknologi tepat guna. Training tentang proper use of fertilizers, seeds, pesticides, dan farm equipment optimization. Development of farmer input supply chains connecting communities dengan reliable suppliers (potentially including Astra Agro distributors). Documentation & mapping farmer seed/input needs. Supports Astra Agro's agricultural product market expansion & farmer customer development. (SDGs 2, 8)

🤝 3. Farmer Cooperative & Supply Chain Development

Program pemberdayaan farmer cooperatives mencakup: organizational strengthening, member training, quality assurance, collective marketing, agroindustry linkages, serta market channel development. Direct engagement dengan Astra Agro untuk identifying supply partnership opportunities dengan farmer groups. Documentation & capacity building untuk meeting Astra Agro supplier standards. Develops farmer organizational infrastructure supporting long-term supply partnerships. (SDGs 1, 8, 10, 17)

Nilai Strategis Partnership untuk Astra Agro

Partnership memberikan Astra Agro: (1) Farmer Supply Development - systematic approach untuk identifying & developing farmer suppliers dalam new geographic regions, building long-term supply relationships, (2) Technology Transfer & Adoption - community-based approach ensures sustainable adoption of improved agricultural practices & Astra Agro products at grassroots level, (3) Supply Chain Stability - farmer cooperative development reduces supply chain risks through organized farmer networks meeting quality & volume requirements, (4) Food Security Positioning - tangible contribution to Indonesia's food security agenda supporting Astra Agro's corporate positioning as "Nation's Agriculture Partner", (5) Market Intelligence - deep community engagement provides valuable insights about local agricultural needs, crop preferences, & market opportunities. Investment CSR Rp 300-400 juta delivers strategic ROI: 500+ farmers trained, 5+ farmer cooperatives developed, secured supply pipelines, enhanced sustainability metrics, competitive advantage dalam farmer development & sustainable agriculture.

Terima kasih atas perhatian PT Astra Agro Lestari. Kami yakin partnership ini akan memperkuat Astra Agro's positioning sebagai leader dalam sustainable agriculture development sambil menciptakan dampak sosial berkelanjutan bagi komunitas petani di wilayah 3T.
