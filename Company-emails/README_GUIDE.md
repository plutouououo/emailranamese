## ⚠️ Common Objections & Response Strategy

### **Objection:** "Kami sudah punya program CSR sendiri di region lain"
**Response:** "Absolutely! KKN provides an excellent opportunity untuk expand impact ke new regions. Kami menyediakan ground presence & community network - Anda menyediakan funding & expertise. Win-win partnership."

### **Objection:** "Budget CSR kami sudah allocated"
**Response:** "Kami bisa flexible dengan timing. Bisa untuk FY2026 atau bahkan phase-in approach (Phase 1 di Q2 2026, Phase 2 di Q3). Mari discuss timeline yang sesuai budget cycle perusahaan."

### **Objection:** "Bagaimana dengan sustainability setelah KKN selesai?"
**Response:** "Great question! Kami design programs dengan exit strategy - transfer knowledge, strengthen local organizations, establish community self-sustaining systems. Corporate support bisa evolve menjadi long-term partnership."

### **Objection:** "Kami lebih suka direct CSR implementation daripada partner dengan universitas"
**Response:** "Understood. UGM partnership actually reduces risk & increases impact. Kami provide academic rigor, monitoring, reporting - kualitas terjamin. Universitas leverage existing relationships & networks. Better together."

### **Follow-up Escalation:**
- **3 days after email:** No response = Check email delivery
- **1 week after email:** No response = Make phone call
- **2 weeks after email:** No response = Mark "Low Priority" untuk future follow-up
- **Positive response:** Schedule meeting within 3 days

## ✨ Best Practices

### **Do's:**
- ✅ Personalize every email (at minimum company name, correct CSR person name)
- ✅ Send at right time (Tues-Thurs, morning ideally)
- ✅ Follow up within 3-5 days if no response
- ✅ Be prepared for meetings with clear value prop & budget breakdown
- ✅ Show genuine interest dalam their CSR priorities
- ✅ Propose flexible partnership structures (not one-size-fits-all)

### **Don'ts:**
- ❌ Send generic mass emails (kills credibility)
- ❌ Contact wrong person (CSR person ≠ CEO ≠ Foundation head)
- ❌ Be too pushy in follow-ups
- ❌ Make promises you can't deliver
- ❌ Ignore their stated budget/timeline constraints
- ❌ Forget to thank them for time/consideration
