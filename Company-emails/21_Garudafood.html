KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise Partnership

Yth. Pimpinan PT Garudafood,

Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengundang Garudafood berkolaborasi dalam food security dan rural distributor network expansion program di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Partnership meningkatkan akses produk makanan bergizi sambil membangun sustainable distributor base di 8 villages.

📄 Lihat Proposal

GEMA SEHAT & ARTA NIAGA Nutrition + Distribution Partnership

Nutrition awareness campaign meningkatkan brand exposure Garudafood ke 3000+ community members, dengan training 50 women entrepreneurs sebagai distributor lokal. Community food security program menjangkau 1000+ households dengan Garudafood product integration, creating sustainable supply chain dari village level.

Manfaat Garudafood: (1) 50 trained distributor entrepreneurs creating Rp 2B+ annual COGS from region, (2) 3000+ consumer awareness building foundation untuk Garudafood portfolio, (3) Rural market penetration dengan authentic nutrition education & brand trust, (4) Distributor network asset creation untuk future scale-up di NTT. FMCG market expansion infrastructure.

Terima kasih. Kami tunggu kolaborasi untuk nutrition security dan rural marketplace development.
