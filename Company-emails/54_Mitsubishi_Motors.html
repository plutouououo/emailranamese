Kami dari KKN-PPM UGM Rana Mese Manise mengajak Mitsubishi Motors untuk partnership dalam automotive technician training & transportation services development di Manggarai Timur, NTT. Program fokus pada vocational mechanic training, rural transport services, & automotive skill creation.

LENTERA PELITA - Automotive Technician & Transportation Skills Training

Automotive mechanics training program melatih 130 youth dalam Mitsubishi vehicle repair, maintenance, & diagnostic skills dengan international standard certification. Service center network establishing 130 certified mechanics dengan Mitsubishi authorized service partnership. Monthly service volume creating Rp 2.8+ billion annual aftermarket revenue dengan sustainable mechanic employment & rural automotive infrastructure.

Manfaat Mitsubishi: (1) 130 certified Mitsubishi mechanics creating service network & aftermarket revenue stream in region, (2) Rp 2.8B+ annual service volume creating brand presence & customer loyalty in NTT market, (3) Youth employment & vocational skills documentation strengthening Mitsubishi youth development CSR, (4) Rural automotive infrastructure enabling Mitsubishi vehicle penetration & market support. Vocational training & automotive infrastructure expansion.
