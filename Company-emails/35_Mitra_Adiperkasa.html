ARTA NIAGA - Retail Distribution & Merchant Network

Retail network expansion training melatih 70 retail entrepreneurs & warung owners dengan inventory management, POS process, & product merchandising. Merchant empowerment program establishing 70 last-mile retail touchpoints creating consumer goods accessibility untuk 8 villages. Retail sales target Rp 3.5+ billion monthly dari Mitra Adiperkasa distribution network dengan competitive pricing & product assortment.

Manfaat Mitra Adiperkasa: (1) 70 trained retail partners creating Rp 3.5B+ monthly sales & sustainable last-mile distribution network, (2) Market penetration into underserved NTT region with established merchant base & supply chain, (3) Retail network asset creation extending Mitra Adiperkasa distribution reach & consumer accessibility, (4) Merchant empowerment documentation strengthening retail sector ESG narrative. Distribution infrastructure expansion in East Indonesia.
