## COMMUNICATION PROTOCOLS

### Email Communication:
- **Response SLA:** Commit untuk reply within 24 hours
- **Tone:** Professional, respectful, enthusiastic but not pushy
- **Frequency:** Max 3 outreach emails sebelum consider company as "not interested"
- **Personalization:** Always customize, never send generic mass email

### Phone Communication:
- **Best Timing:** 10-11am atau 2-3pm (working hours)
- **Pitch Duration:** Keep to 2-3 minutes, then ask untuk longer discussion
- **Follow-up:** Send email summary dalam 24 hours
- **Escalation:** If no luck dengan CSR person, try local office head

### LinkedIn:
- **Initial Contact:** Send when sending email (increase visibility)
- **Follow-up:** After 1 week no response, send LinkedIn message
- **Value Add:** Share relevant articles/insights dari company's CSR focus

## COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

### "We don't have budget for new CSR partnerships now"

**Response:** "We understand budget constraints. Bagaimana kalau kita explore alternative contribution types - in-kind support, expertise, produktu trial, atau resource loaning? Flexibility kami sangat tinggi untuk align dengan [Company Name]'s current capacity."

### "Our CSR fokus di area lain/berbeda dengan program Anda"

**Response:** "I appreciate that. Namun lihat bahwa [specific aspect of program kami] closely align dengan [specific CSR focus Anda]. Plus, community-integrated approach kami bisa provide valuable insights untuk expansion CSR [Company Name]. Bisa we discuss specific areas dimana [Company Name] paling comfortable contribute?"

### "Kami sudah punya CSR partnership lain di lokasi serupa"

**Response:** "That's great! Kami actually open untuk collaborative approach dengan existing partners. Maybe [Company Name]'s experience di area tsb bisa enhance program kami further? Or kami explore cross-organization synergy?"

### "Kita butuh discuss dengan board/leadership first"

**Response:** "Absolutely. Kami understand decision making process. Kami ready to present ke leadership whenever convenient. Apa timeline target untuk keputusan ini? Kami bisa adjust program timeline untuk accommodate [Company Name]'s process."

### "Timeline terlalu tight, program dimulai June"

**Response:** "I understand June terasa cepat. Namun partnership preparation kami bisa streamlined untuk quick deployment. Plus, contribution bisa phased - intensive involvement dimulai June, tapi preliminary discussion/planning bisa start now. Feasible kah?"
