## CRITICAL TIPS FOR EMAIL SENDING

1. **Personalize** - Use nama kontak actual, mention specific CSR programs, show you did research
2. **Be Specific** - Jangan generic. Show you understand company's current CSR focus
3. **Show Mutual Benefit** - Jelas articulate benefit untuk company, bukan hanya community
4. **Include Attachment** - Always attach proposal, tidak hanya describe
5. **Professional Format** - Use company letterhead, proper email signatures
6. **Follow Up** - Don't give up setelah first email. Follow up strategically
7. **Multiple Contacts** - Cc relevant stakeholders (CSR head, Sustainability officer, local office)
